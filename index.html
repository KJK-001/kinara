About Us

Kinara Services is a digital solutions hub committed to empowering individuals, students, and institutions through ethical ICT practices. We believe that technology should simplify life, not complicate it — and our mission is to bridge the gap between people and the services they need most.

Our team specializes in government applications, career documentation, and creative branding. Whether you are applying for HELB, registering a business, or preparing professional CVs and reports, we provide step‑by‑step guidance to ensure accuracy, compliance, and efficiency. We combine technical expertise with a human touch, making every process seamless and reliable.

Beyond documentation and government services, Kinara Services is also a creative hub. We design posters, event cards, and branding materials that help institutions and entrepreneurs stand out. Our approach blends innovation with professionalism, ensuring that every project we deliver makes a lasting impact.

At Kinara Services, integrity is at the heart of everything we do. We are driven by clarity, transparency, and a commitment to excellence. By choosing us, you gain a trusted partner who values your goals and works tirelessly to help you achieve them.

Knowledge Hub

Step-by-step guides to help you navigate essential services.

  1. Visit the HELB Portal.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Fill in the loan application form carefully.
  4. Upload required documents (ID, admission letter, guarantor details).
  5. Submit and print the loan application form for signing.
  1. Go to the KRA iTax Portal.
  2. Click “New PIN Registration.”
  3. Fill in your personal details (ID, DOB, email).
  4. Submit and download your PIN certificate.
  1. Visit eCitizen.
  2. Create an account with your ID number.
  3. Log in and select the service you need (passport, licence, etc.).
  4. Fill in the form and pay online via M-Pesa or card.
  5. Download your receipt and follow instructions for collection.
  1. Go to KUCCPS Portal.
  2. Log in with your KCSE index number and year.
  3. Select preferred courses and universities.
  4. Pay the application fee via M-Pesa.
  5. Submit and wait for placement results.
  1. Visit the official SHA Portal.
  2. Create an account using your National ID number and a valid email address.
  3. Log in to your account and select “Register for SHA.”
  4. Fill in your personal details (ID, date of birth, contact information).
  5. Upload required documents (ID copy, proof of residence, or employer details if applicable).
  6. Submit your application and download the confirmation slip.
  7. You will receive a notification once your SHA membership is activated.
